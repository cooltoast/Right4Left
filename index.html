YOUR RIGHT FOR LEFT-HANDEDNESS

I learned to play tennis left-handed a few years ago. The idea came to me when my right arm was ravaged by poison ivy. I began playing with my left hand and after a few months, I could beat my older brother.

Welcome to Right4Left.com! My name is Markan Patel, and whether you injured your right arm or have tennis elbow, I will help you get back on the court, because everyone has the right for left-handedness.
