Welcome to Right4Left.com!

My name is Markan Patel, and I have learned to play tennis left-handed a few years ago. The idea came to me when my right arm was ravaged by poison ivy. I began playing left-handed tennis and after a few months, I could beat my brother.

Whether you broke your arm or you have tennis elbow, Right4Left.com will help you get back on the court, because everyone has the right to left-handedness.
